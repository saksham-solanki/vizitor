How Vizitor can help you at your workplace?

Contact Tracing

Amidst this lockdown, the biggest worry is getting exposed to the virus. Vizitor helps you with contact tracing of customers coming in your salon. So you can be sure that your employees and visitors both are safe.

Custom Health Screening Questionnaire

Vizitor allows you to customize your healthcare screening questions. You can also customize these questions as per your needs.

Schedule Appointments

Vizitor provides a consistent and branded experience to your customers by providing them with an option to schedule appointments online from the comfort of their homes.

Reduce No-Shows

Vizitor sends out automated email and SMS reminders to your customers about their appointment, you can also notify your customers if there is a change in their appointment. Visitor Is the best solution for Salons and Spas.
