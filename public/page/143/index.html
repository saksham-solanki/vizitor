Effortless Check-in Solution!

  • Digital Document Signing
    Easily review and sign NDAs, waivers, or agreements digitally during check-in. Vizitor’s paperless document signing keeps sensitive information secure, eliminates manual paperwork, and ensures faster onboarding.
  • Instant Visitor Badge Printing
    Create professional visitor badges in seconds, complete with name, photo, and access details. Badges enhance workplace security, improve visitor tracking, and give every guest a professional first impression.
  • Touchless / QR Code Check-In
    Experience safe, contactless visitor check-in by simply scanning a QR code. This fast, hygienic process minimizes waiting times, reduces manual entry errors, and enhances workplace safety.
  • Visitor Pre-Registration
    Allow guests to pre-register before arrival for a smooth entry experience. Pre-registration enables hosts to approve visitors in advance, skip front-desk delays, and maintain a secure visitor log.
