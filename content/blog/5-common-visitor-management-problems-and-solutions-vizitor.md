Traditional methods, such as paper logs and manual check-ins, often lead to inefficiencies, security risks, and compliance challenges.

## **1. Slow and Inefficient Check-In Processes**

### **The Problem**

Manual check-in processes can be time-consuming and prone to errors. Visitors often have to fill out forms by hand, leading to long wait times and potential inaccuracies in the recorded information. This not only frustrates visitors but also strains front desk staff and compromises security.

### **How Vizitor Solves It**

**Vizitor** streamlines the check-in process with features like **QR code scanning**, **pre-registration**, and **mobile check-ins**. Visitors can register in advance, reducing wait times and ensuring accurate data collection.

**Key Benefits:**

* **Faster check-ins**: Reduce visitor wait times and improve overall efficiency.
* **Accurate data collection**: Minimize errors associated with manual entry.
* **Enhanced visitor experience**: Create a professional and welcoming first impression.



## **2. Security Risks and Unauthorized Access**

### **The Problem**

Without proper visitor management systems, unauthorized individuals may gain access to restricted areas, posing security risks. Manual logs are often inadequate for tracking visitor movements and ensuring compliance with security protocols.

### **How Vizitor Solves It**

**Vizitor** enhances security by offering **customizable access levels**, **visitor badges**, and **real-time monitoring**. Administrators can set permissions based on visitor type, ensuring that individuals only access areas relevant to their visit.

**Key Benefits:**

* **Controlled access**: Restrict entry to authorized areas only.
* **Real-time monitoring**: Track visitor movements and activities.
* **Improved compliance**: Adhere to security protocols and regulations.
