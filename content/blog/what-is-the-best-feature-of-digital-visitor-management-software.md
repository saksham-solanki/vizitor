## Digital Document Signing- NDA’s

Enhance the security of your office by making visitors to sign NDAs digitally. If your legal document is required, no visitor will be able to sign in without providing their signature.

Also called a “confidentiality agreement,” a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) protects your company’s confidential information. An NDA is useful any time you want to share information

with someone, but want to make sure that they don’t share it with anyone else. Here are a few examples of when you might use an NDA:


• Sharing information with a potential business partner or vendor

• Pitching a new product or technology to investors

• Giving employees access to sensitive information that they need to do their jobs
