## Key Features to Look for in a Smart  Meeting Room Scheduler

* Real-Time Availability: Make sure the scheduler provides up-to-date room availability to prevent booking conflicts.
* Integration with Calendar Tools: A seamless connection with Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, or other tools can simplify scheduling for employees.
* Automated Reminders and Notifications: To ensure attendees are reminded of their meetings and avoid last-minute rushes.
* Customizable Room Preferences: Features such as room capacity, amenities (projectors, Wi-Fi), and layout preferences should be easily customizable.
* Analytics and Reports: Insightful data on room usage patterns, peak times, and booking frequency to optimize the meeting room system.
