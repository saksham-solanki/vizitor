### 3. Define Clear Booking Rules: Set Expectations

Imagine the situation when an employee books a room for an hour-long meeting but leaves after just 15 minutes. The room remains unoccupied for the next 45 minutes while others scramble to find space.



Establishing clear rules for using meeting rooms is essential for avoiding conflicts and ensuring accountability. Display these policies prominently in your meeting room management software so that everyone follows the same guidelines.



Policies to Consider:

* Maximum booking time per user (e.g., 2 hours).
* Penalties for no-shows to discourage misuse.
* Rules for recurring bookings to ensure fair usage.



💡 Pro Tip: Include an option in the booking system to report misuse or suggest improvements, fostering a sense of shared responsibility.
