### 1. Ensuring Data Accuracy with Automated Attendance

Accurate attendance records are the foundation of effective workforce management. Inaccurate attendance can lead to payroll errors, mismanagement of employee schedules, and even compliance issues. Traditional methods, such as manual time logging or outdated systems, are prone to errors due to human intervention. This often results in discrepancies, such as incorrect overtime calculations, proxy attendance (someone marking attendance for a colleague), and confusion regarding actual working hours.

With online attendance tracking, these errors are dramatically reduced. By automating the attendance process, businesses ensure the highest level of accuracy, and Vizitor takes it a step further by offering a QR-based system that is integrated with GPS technology.

Vizitor’s GPS-based system ensures that attendance is recorded accurately based on the employee’s location. For example, an employee must be physically present at the office or designated worksite for the system to mark them as ‘present.’ This reduces the possibility of proxy entries and ensures data authenticity.

#### 99.9% Accuracy in Attendance Tracking

Studies show that 99.9% accuracy is achievable through automated systems like Vizitor, which use advanced technologies such as QR scanning and location tracking. With these innovations, businesses can trust that their attendance records are reliable and audit-proof, making it easier to reconcile data for payroll, project tracking, and compliance audits.
