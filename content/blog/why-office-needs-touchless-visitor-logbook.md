### How Touchless visitor management helps you!

### #Keeps workplace healthy

Touchless visitor management system helps to stop the spread of the virus. This system enhances the productivity of the workplace and keeps the workplace healthy. Visitors can check-in without any touch via QR code.

### **#Contact Tracing**

Vizitor provides the facility to track the visitors. You can know when people come and go from your workplace. The contact tracing system provides both visitors and employees with check-in and check-out with adding their information, capturing a photo with the user-friendly interface.

### **#Alert Employees**

The Touchless check-in system sends notifications to the worker when any visitor comes up to satisfy them and alerts them by sending the small print of the guest to form them aware and take necessary precautionary steps. The Touchless visitor management system allows the worker to approve or disapprove the entry of any visitor on the premises.

## **How touchless visitor management works!**

Vizitor, let you welcome your visitors and employees with a contactless check-in. Your visitors and employees can simply check-in with the help of their mobile phones.

1. **Touchless Check-in:-** Harness the power of Touchless Check-in with vizitor. You can simply check-in by scanning a QR code with your mobile phone leading you to a customized form to fill in details.

2. **Enter your details:-** After scanning the QR code a customized form will pop up. Just fill in the required details and submit the form to check-in.
3. **Photo Capture:-** For enhanced security ask your visitor for a photo. You don’t need any additional hardware to capture photos. Visitors can simply capture their photo with their mobile phone.
4. **Health Screening Form:-** Once a visitor has submitted all the required details you can ask them to fill a Health Screening Form right on their mobile phone. This let’s you understand the health condition of your visitors.
5. **e-Pass:-** After filling out all the required details visitors and employees will be issued an E-Pass which will let them enter the facility with ease.
