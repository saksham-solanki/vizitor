## What is Facility Management?

[Facility management](https://www.vizitorapp.com/blog/the-best-features-of-facility-management-system/) is the coordination of physical assets, buildings, and infrastructure to ensure that they operate smoothly, safely, and efficiently. 



It involves everything from daily operations and maintenance to long-term planning and improvements. It’s a multidisciplinary field that encompasses various aspects such as:



* Maintenance management
* Energy management
* Safety and security
* Space planning
* Environmental sustainability

### 3.Embrace Preventive Maintenance for Both Facilities and Visitors

Preventive maintenance isn’t just about equipment and infrastructure—it also includes systems that affect visitors' experience. From restrooms to elevators, ensuring these are always in top condition creates a welcoming atmosphere for your visitors.



Use [facility management software](https://www.vizitorapp.com/blog/the-best-features-of-facility-management-system/) to automate maintenance schedules and integrate visitor-facing elements (e.g., waiting areas, visitor badges, and reception counters) into a seamless process.



 This ensures both facility readiness and a smooth visitor experience.



### 4.Prioritize Energy Management While Enhancing Visitor Comfort

Energy management is essential for reducing operational costs and promoting sustainability. But it also plays a role in visitor comfort.

 Ensuring optimal lighting, HVAC, and temperature settings in visitor-facing areas enhances their experience.



Statistic: Buildings that focus on energy management can reduce energy costs by up to 30%, according to the [U.S. Department of Energy.](https://www.energy.gov/)



💡Use your facility management software to automate energy-efficient practices in visitor spaces—such as adjusting lighting and temperature settings in meeting rooms and waiting areas—ensuring visitor comfort and reducing energy wastage.



### 5. Keep Communication Transparent

[Clear communication](https://www.vizitorapp.com/instant-notification/) between the facility management team, employees, and visitors is essential. Ensuring that everyone is aware of maintenance schedules, safety protocols, and operational changes helps in smooth day-to-day operations.



### 6. Effective Workspace Management 

[Effective maintenance management](https://www.vizitorapp.com/visitor-management-system-for-co-working/) is crucial for preventing operational disruptions and minimizing financial losses. 



Proper maintenance ensures that resources are utilized efficiently, avoiding waste. 



Various software programs can assist in the maintenance process, with key objectives including controlling efficiency, reducing costs, managing work schedules, and ensuring regulatory compliance.



### 7. Gain Clear Insights into Facility Operations

It can be incredibly frustrating not knowing where your time, money, and resources are going on a daily basis. 

With facility management software, you gain complete visibility into completed work orders, assets that have been repaired or replaced, and pending preventative maintenance tasks.
