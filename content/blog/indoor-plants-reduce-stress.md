When you think of office environment, what the first thing that comes into you mind? Very likely we can find equipment's such as computers and printers, furniture. The one thing you probably don’t think of is planting an indoor plants in offices.

But years of [research](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3072911/) says that plants in the workplace have a number of benefits besides being aesthetically pleasing. Plants have many benefits in employees health as well as its improve quality of air, reduces the stress level, decreases the sick days, thereby increasing productivity. Planting is the great way to bolster your productivity and add some cheer to your office by adding some leafy friends around your workplace. They are just like office snacks, office plants are a cost-effective way to brighten up your team's workday and even improve productivity.

## **The Benefits of Office Plants**

Interior plants offer businesses a simultaneously natural and beautiful addition to their interior while benefiting the overall health of employees. By incorporating plant life into the design of your space, your employees will be able to enjoy nature’s beauty and they will enjoy the  physical and psychological benefits of office plants.

**Cleanses Air Quality**

As we know, plants clean the air by taking in carbon dioxide and other pollutants and releasing oxygen through the process of photosynthesis. Plants naturally filter toxins from the rooms that they grow in and help freshen up the place. In fact, some types of plants are able to remove 50-75% of such chemical compounds such as hexane and benzene, which are found in common cleaning agents.

**Reduces The Stress**

Sometimes stress is unavoidable, even if you love where you work. Office plants have been shown to reduce stress levels in employees when introduced into the workplace. According to study conducted, found that offices that were spruced up with plants saw the following benefits:

- **37% reduction in anxiety.**
- **44% reduction in office hostility.**

- **Reduced chronic fatigue by nearly 40%.**
- **58% reduction in reported depression.**

**Plants Absorbs Noises**

The plants absorb the background office chatter. This is especially true if your work space has hard surfaces such as exposed concrete walls or floors, since there are no other means of absorbing the excess noise.
