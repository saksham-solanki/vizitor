The beauty of innovation can be very well explained with improvement and inventions. And without a doubt bug is very much part of this innovation. Talking about innovation, the world is shifting from service based softwares to cloud based. Talking about visitor management is specific, cloud based software are much more convenient. Before we emphasis more on this, let us first understand what do we mean by cloud based? Cloud based refers to applications, services or resources made available to users on customer demands and preferences via the Internet from a cloud computing provider's servers.

When it comes to visitor management software, one should not think twice and select cloud based software. Vizitor is a cloud-based visitor management solution which caters to businesses such as salons, offices, universities, hotels, airports and hospitals. The solution is accessible with Android and iOS devices.

## Reasons why make a shift?

### 1. Free Trials:

 When it comes to such type of services, one must always try it beforehand, like you try cloth or car before you actually pay the amount.

### 2. Simple and Easy: 

Responsibility is as heavy thing and if one is solely responsible to manage data, it is a big task. So for relief, it is better that you shift to cloud based, once synced, you will never lose the data.

### 3. Cost-effective:

 Get rid of papers/registers/pen/pencil and move to paperless and cost effective softwares like vizitor which makes your life easier at the lower cost possible.

### 4. Relationship building:

 Such softwares are impressive, imagine one enters your work space and follows an easy and simple process of check in which consumes less time. It develops better relation.

### 5. Up-to-date:

 The data is updated daily, minute to minute or to be specific second to second! So, it’s a care free solution.

### 6. Accessibility: 

You don’t have to flip pages or look after sheets, you just need good speed internet and you can look for anything you want

### 7.Sustainability: 

This software fills a social responsibility too, it is an environment friendly solution and saves paper.  

So, to conclude one must definitely try it, analyse it and make a decision next.
