## What do you mean by Eco-friendly office?

The Eco-friendly means the earth-friendly which are not harmful for the environment in anyway. Green offices commonly refers to spaces that contribute to the green livings within the offices and cater the practices that are helpful in conserving the resources. No matter the size of your business, there are many common things you can do to impact your operations and enhance your green credibility. Here are just a few:

## Go Green with Internet Of Things

The Internet of things is defined as of Internet connectivity into physical devices and everyday objects. It is true that this connection and interconnection that the Internet of Things has to offer opens the doors to the collection and storage of important data. In cities, towns and countries as well, the Internet of Things can provide a marked efficiency and improve the general quality of life in many different ways. Many companies currently use networked sensors to look at potential problems with their equipment and supply lines and optimize their use of energy to ensure a safer, greener approach.

## Green Machines

Computers and related technologies account for 3-4% of the world's carbon emissions. So changing the type of computers your company is using can make a difference. If your company is purchasing new machines, make sure you check out its ethical and environmental friendliness. Also, choose hardware that uses less toxic chemicals in the manufacturing process.
