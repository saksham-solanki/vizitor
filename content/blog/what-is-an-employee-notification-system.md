## What is an Employee Notification System?

An [employee notification system](https://www.vizitorapp.com/instant-notification/) is a communication tool designed to send urgent or important messages to employees via multiple channels. These messages can be delivered through:

* Pop-up notifications on desktops
* Email alerts
* Text messages (SMS)
* Push notifications on mobile devices
* Loudspeakers and PA systems
* Digital signage

By using these communication methods, organizations can ensure that their employees receive real-time updates, irrespective of their location.

## Comparison:  Traditional Communication Methods Vs Modern Notification Systems 

Many businesses still rely on outdated communication methods like email, notice boards, and manual calls. These methods are often slow and inefficient.



Emails vs. Push Notifications:



* The average email open rate is only 20%, meaning that important messages may go unread.
* Push notifications, on the other hand, have a 90% open rate within 3 minutes of being sent.



Bulletin Boards vs. Instant Messaging:



* Bulletin boards require employees to be physically present to read announcements.
