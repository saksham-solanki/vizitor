## **Tips for Staying Calm During Emergencies**

Emergencies can be overwhelming. Here are simple steps to respond effectively:

* **Stay calm** and breathe before speaking.
* **Share your exact location** (nearby landmarks help).
* **Explain what happened** briefly but clearly.
* **Follow instructions** given by the responder.
* **Do not hang up** until they confirm they have the information they need.

# **FAQs**

### **1. Are these emergency numbers valid across all of India?**

Yes, numbers like **112, 100, 101, 102, 1091, and 1098** are national helplines. However, certain services may differ by state.

### **2. Will the responders assist even if I call by mistake?**

If you dial accidentally, stay on the line and inform them so the call is not treated as an emergency.

### **3. Can these numbers be dialed without mobile balance?**

Yes, most emergency numbers work even if your phone has zero balance.

### **4. Do these helpline numbers work 24/7?**

Yes, all listed emergency services operate round-the-clock.

### **5. How can I teach kids to use emergency numbers safely?**

Explain basic rules:

* Dial only during a real emergency
* Stay calm
* Share name, location, and what happened
