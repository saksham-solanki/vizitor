## What Exactly is Meeting Room Analytics?

Meeting room analytics refers to the process of collecting and analyzing data about the utilization of meeting spaces within an office. It leverages advanced tools like:



* Sensors (occupancy and motion sensors).
* [Meeting room booking systems](https://www.vizitorapp.com/meeting-room-booking-system/) integrated with calendar tools.
* Conference room scheduling software for detailed reporting.

The goal is to provide actionable insights on meeting room usage patterns, including:



* Frequency of bookings.
* Room occupancy versus capacity.
* Technology and equipment preferences.
* Peak usage hours.



This data enables businesses to design smarter, more efficient workspaces that align with employee needs.
