## **What Is Visitor Management?**

**Visitor management** is the process of monitoring, recording, and controlling who enters your workplace. It ensures that every visitor: vendors, guests, clients, mechanics, delivery agents, consultants, etc is properly verified, recorded, and supervised.

Modern organisations use **digital visitor management systems (VMS)** instead of paper logs to automate:

* Visitor registration
* ID verification
* Host notifications
* Visitor badge printing
* Access control
* Real-time tracking
* Emergency evacuation lists

A well-designed visitor entry system ensures security, compliance, and a smoother visitor experience.

## **What Is a Visitor Management System (VMS)?**

A **visitor management system** is software that replaces paper logs with a secure, automated digital check-in process.

A VMS typically includes tools for:

* **Visitor pre-registration**
* **QR code–based visitor entry**
* **Self-service check-ins via tablet/mobile**
* **Automatic visitor badge printing**
* **Visitor registration forms**
* **Live visitor tracking**
* **Digital NDAs and consent forms**
* **Access control integrations**
* **Real-time notifications to hosts**
