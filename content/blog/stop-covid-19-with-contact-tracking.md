## A close look at the tracing app's benefits:

The Indian Governments have set out a plan to remove lockdown and want to get people back to work by using digital tools. **Contact-tracing technology** is one that helps authorities track the virus and warn staff with consistent alerts. Here are some ways to protect the workplaces with this tool.

### Contact tracing is a safe way to keep the correct record

- This app facilities record the data in a safe and hygienic way to capture the information.

- The contact tracing system provides both visitors and employees with check-in and check-out with adding their information, capturing a photo with the user-friendly interface.

### Visitor screening

- With the screening feature, You can screen the visitors before their entry on the premises.
- This app uses visitor initial screening with digital questionnaires asking their health and travel history.

- Screening determines whether they are safe to enter your business or not. Employees can approve or disapprove visitors with the screening using this application.

### Contactless visitor check-in

- Contactless check-ins are the best practice to prevent the spread of infection in the workplace.
- Visitors use their smartphones for themselves check-ins without the help of any third person.

- Sanitizing the tablet and smartphones before using.
- Visitors can check-ins using **QR code**.

### Automatic Alerts

- **The contact-tracing system** helps to track the infected person and warn staff with alerts.

- The contact tracing app notifies the employee if they have been in contact with an infected visitor.

## Use of contact tracing system for every sector

This app is specially designed for offices, industries, hospitals, and many other premises that are planning to re-open offices after lockdown. Here are some ways to protect every sector with this system.

### For Healthcare Sector:

Contact tracing is one of the most effective tools that public health officials have to halt a pandemic and prevent future outbreaks.

- It can assist the healthcare sector to screen the visitors and prevent the spread of this outbreak.

- Replaces the manual contact tracing to **contactless** operations.

### For Offices:    

New coronavirus contact tracing tool could help offices reopen by tracing employees.

- Visitor screening:- Easily screen visitors by asking them questions.

- Instant Alerts:- Notification to the employees for a arrival of any infected person

### For Industries:

Industries like manufacturing, pharmaceutical units are considered an “*essential services*” organization today, so Contact Tracing tool act as saviours during this pandemic.

- Visitor Screening before entering with No-touch thermometers

- Touchless check-ins 

### **For Government:**

Government agencies already use investigative tools for locating and contacting individuals who may be involved in social services, tax fraud or criminal investigations.

- Time Stamping with visitor records including date, name.

- The alerts are generated by scanning through government-owned, location-specific databases
