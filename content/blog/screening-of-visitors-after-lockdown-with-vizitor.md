As we know, We are now in the second month of the lockdown in India. The virus outbreak in India has also hit the country’s economy and services. Currently, Most companies do not have the processes to support their employees in this situation. 

With every visitor walking in through the front desk, Companies are feeling the threat of disease entering their premises and infecting their employees. Many Countries follow the guidelines of their government and trying to make the normalcy. Like, In Hong-Kong, normalcy is coming. People are going out again in the workplace, shopping again, walking on the streets again but with the major guidelines.

**What is the new normal in Hong-Kong and how can we learn from them?**

1. They came back to the office in shifts, not every one altogether.
2. They all fill the daily health-check surveys online.
3. When they enter the office, they check their temperatures and wear masks. They mask up in public spaces as well.
4. They follow social protocols.
5. They already have increased the hygiene level.

So when we will be going back to the office after lock down, we should follow the guidelines told by our government. We should use and implement these guidelines on our premises too. Avoid the **hotspot area**, follow the **social protocol**, **avoid social gathering**.

**So, what should we do when employees go back to their offices?**

**1.Check-Points at the Entrance and Screening the visitors with Vizitor**

Do the Screening of the employees at the main gate and entry points. If anyone found to have a fever,  cough, cold should be politely told that they cannot be allowed to enter the workplace. Use infra-red thermometers to measure body temperature.
