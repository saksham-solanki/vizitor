### #2. Full risk assessment with Screening and Alerts

As we return to the  workplace. To prevent the spread of Covid-19 , the organization has to ensure the full risk assessment before permitted entry of any visitor. ### #4. Make your front-desk process Contactless

As we now know limiting human contact is one sure way to slow down the spread of Covid-19. So make your workplace Contactless. Making workplace contact less is the great way to prevent the Covid-19.
