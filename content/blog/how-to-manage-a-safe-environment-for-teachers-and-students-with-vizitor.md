## **# Why do schools need a visitor management system?**

After the pandemic, the safety of the students and staff is important to parents and a school management.So therefore, it is important to keep track and screening of people entering and visiting children during school hours.

{{< figure src="/images/blog/image-182.png" >}}



### **->Here are some of the limitations of a paper-based visitor log book.**

In today's digital world, Many schools are still using a paper-based visitor log book to keep records of all their visitors. But this system is not safe enough and it has many limitations:

- the records are not safe that stored in a book are difficult to manage and search
- the records are stored in a physical form which is prone to damage and loss

- there are no ways of sharing the records without physically coping them
- the data stored on log books are accessible by the  theft and non-compliance
- Health  Visitors Screening  is impossible using a log book
- During an emergency, visitor log books are not effective.
- unauthorized person can be enter the school

Because of these all reasons, many schools still use them to record visitors' information. This is not an efficient way of managing school safety during the pandemic.
