A [digital clock in and out system](https://www.vizitorapp.com/visitor-check-in-systems/) automates employee attendance tracking using modern technology such as biometrics, mobile apps, RFID cards, and geolocation tracking. 



Unlike traditional manual registers or punch cards, digital systems offer real-time tracking, payroll automation, and security enhancements.



Step-by-Step Process of a Digital Clock In and Out System

### 1. Employee Authentication & Check-In

Employees can clock in using different methods:

✔ Biometric Login – Fingerprint, facial recognition, or retina scan to prevent time fraud.

✔ Mobile App Clock-In – Employees use their phones to log in with GPS tracking & geofencing.

✔ RFID Card Swipe – Employees scan their ID cards at an access point.

✔ Web-Based Login – Employees log in through a secure online portal from any device.



### 2. Geo-Fencing & Location Tracking (For Remote Workers)

✔ Restricts clock-ins only to designated work areas.

✔ Ensures field employees clock in at the right location.

### 3.  Real-Time Work Hour Recording & Timesheet Generation
