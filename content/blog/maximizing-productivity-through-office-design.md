## 1.Consider your Office Layout 

According to a study,workplace design can improve productivity by up to 20%.
When you are thinking about the best way to design your office space, it is important to consider how the layout will impact the productivity level of your employees and  how it can encourage collaboration, streamline workflows at the workplace.

Every business and building will have unique considerations, so it's important to carefully design the workspace to suit your specific needs. By doing so, you can create an environment that helps your team work more efficiently and effectively.

## 2. Elevate your Front Office Space

Your front desk sets the tone of your guests' every visit. So,when you think of enhancing your brand efficiency,then the first and the foremost thing is to optimize your front desk space.The importance of making your front desk more appealing cannot be overlooked at any cost to make your office design more authentic.

Make your front office more attractive and functional to create a positive first impression to streamline your visitor flow.
What all you can do to optimize your Office space? Here are some tips to consider–

### 2.1 Remove Unnecessary Files

A cluttered office space can create distractions and inconvenience for both visitors and employees.So,keep it as less as possible.If your office is still relying upon manual paper-based front desk operations, then consider digitizing your registration process by making your guests register in advance or using their mobile devices. 

Investing in a right **[visitor management system](/best-visitor-management-system-in-india)** like Vizitor can make your frontal staff free from registering visitors manually on paper logs. 

Don't create mess at your doorsteps by placing heaps of unnecessary papers and files. It is important to keep your space minimal and clutter-free.

### 2.2 Prioritize natural lighting

Your guests and employees spend much of their time inside office space, so it is important to design an office with adequate and open  windows to make  natural light  pass through them. 

If this isn't possible, you may  install light bulbs rated to omit natural light inside your premises.
Proper lighting can help your team to stay focused and reduce eye strain from fluorescent and unnatural lights.

If possible, choose an office space with high ceilings. This will create a sense of openness and airiness that can boost mood and productivity.


### 2.3 Keep your space organized 

Desks, tables, chairs and other equipment take much of your office space and may create much  distraction and discomfort. Keep only the essential items in your space and ensure that your workspace is clean and organized.
After all, gone are the days of closed-off office spaces.Modern Office designs prioritize open floors and minimal equipment requirements. 

Eliminate the need of printers  and file cabinets for organizing registration forms and visitor passes.Instead utilize QR codes or signs that visitors can scan with their smartphones to access information or complete forms online.


### 2.4 Comfortable seating options 

Another major factor to consider in modern office design is Comfort. Using comfortable and flexible furniture at the front desk improves functionality and flexibility of your workspace and also creates  a more welcoming environment for visitors.

Incorporate soft seating chairs and furniture that can be adjusted accordingly to support good posture and reduce the risk of discomfort and injury.

Invest in modern and modular seating options for creating flexible seating arrangements.It may help alleviate physical stressors such as eye strain and back pain, which can be distracting and negatively impact workplace productivity.

### 2.5 Choose the right color 

The colors around us can affect our mood, which can impact productivity. Choosing the right and calming colors for your workspace that can boost your productivity and reduce stress.
When choosing colors for a modern office design, consider the company's brand, how it will affect employee productivity, and the overall vibe of the workspace.

## 4. Prioritize Green Practices in your Design

Designing an eco-friendly office is rapidly becoming a popular trend in office design, as it offers numerous benefits to both the workplace and the environment. Introducing green practices while designing office space not only improves productivity and your business’s image but also saves the environment.

### 4.1 Get some indoor plants 

A study found that employees who have plants in their workspace are 15% more productive than those who don't.
Get some plants for the office that clean carbon dioxide from indoor air, they’re also good at absorbing other chemicals.
The plants  help reduce stress, increase productivity, reduce sick days, and boost creativity.


### 4.2 Choose eco-friendly furniture

Opt for eco-friendly furniture in modern office designs which is made from sustainable materials.
Using neutral colors while choosing furniture such as white, gray, and beige as a base to create a calming and professional atmosphere in the office.

### 4.3 Go Paperless with Cloud Storage 

As the business world is  becoming increasingly competitive, businesses need to adopt modern solutions to stay ahead, and going paperless is essential to achieve it. 

In this digital world, you need to make sure that you should send most of your announcements over email and store files online instead of printing for every single bit of information 

Use cloud-based solutions to store and share digital files securely, reducing the need for storing data manually on paper logs.Cloud storage allows for remote access to documents, enabling employees to work from any location, increasing flexibility and productivity.

Issue Digital Visitor Badges instead of paper ones to eliminate the need for paper and pen and to prioritize sustainability at the workplace.


By incorporating sustainability elements such as energy-efficient lighting, green materials, and sustainable landscaping into your modern office design, you can create a functional and comfortable workplace that meets the needs of your employees and promotes productivity and success.

## 5. Emphasize your branding

Incorporate  your company's branding and design elements into the office space to  create a sense of pride and belonging among employees, leading to increased productivity.

Display your company logo in the office space to create a strong visual association with your brand.
Incorporate your brand's colors into your modern office design to create a cohesive look and feel that reflects your brand's identity.
