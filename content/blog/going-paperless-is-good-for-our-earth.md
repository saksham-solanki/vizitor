## **Here are the benefits of going paperless :-**

Going paperless is a big assessment for the earth. Here are some reasons for why.

#### **1. Save paper Save trees**

The use of paper has increased day by day in the last 40 years. So it's clear this usage takes a huge toll on trees. The result of this is rapid deforestation. This is a huge concern for our environment. As we know Trees are the main parts of our life. They play a critical role to support life on our planet. By eliminating our use of paper, we can do a bit to preserve the life of trees.

{{< figure src="/images/blog/image-289.png" >}}



#### **2. Cut down on pollution**

Yes, a paperless environment can cut down on pollution. As we know that paper manufacturing is one of the worst offenders when it comes to pollution.Did you know making one tonne of paper emits more than 1.5 tonnes of CO2 equivalent? So when we use less of the papers then we can help to reduce the number of pollutants in our atmosphere.

{{< figure src="/images/blog/image-290.png" >}}



#### **3. Save water**

Paperless environment will help to save water. All natural resources can be saved with less usage of the water. Making paper also requires water. Every 1 sheet of paper requires three gallons of water to make. By going paperless, you can help to conserve the water.

{{< figure src="/images/blog/image-291.png" >}}



#### **4.Reduce fuel usage**

Yes you are reading right!! Paperless environment reduces the transport. When a paper-based system every document that is transported across town uses up fuel.  So when the fuel burns then there will be released greenhouse gases. So there is only one solution, just switch to a digital document where a secure and signed document is sent with the click of a button.
