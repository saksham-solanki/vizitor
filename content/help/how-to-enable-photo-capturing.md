Having a digital visitor management system present at your front desk / reception is a blessing and having a photo capturing feature provides an extra layer of security to your organisation.

**Step 1:** Login to your Vizitor dashboard.

**Step 2:** Click on the settings tab from the top panel.

![Locations](/images/help/Locations.svg)

**Step 3:** Click on the configuration tab from the left panel.

![configuration](/images/help/configuration.svg)

**Step 4:** Click on the App setting tab from Left.

![check-in-process](/images/help/check-in-process.svg)

**Step 5:** You can enable or disable the capture visitor photo option by toggling the button to required.
