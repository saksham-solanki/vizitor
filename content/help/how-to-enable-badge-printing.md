In Vizitor, managing badge printing options for visitor check-ins is a breeze. Follow these steps to enable or disable badge printing:

If you are a business that receives 100s of guests or visitors daily then keeping a track of them can be more easy by using a Visitor management system. You can put an extra layer of verification by printing the badges for people who check-in to your premises.

**Step 1:** Access your Vizitor dashboard: Log in to your Vizitor account using your credentials.

**Step 2:** Click on the settings tab from the top panel.

![Locations](/images/help/Locations.svg)


**Step 3:** Click on the configuration tab from the left menu.

![configurationtab](/images/help/configurationtab.svg)

**Step 4:** Click on the App setting tab from top.

**Step 5:** Enable or Disable Badge Printing: In the badge printing settings section, you'll find a toggle button or checkbox that allows you to enable or disable the badge printing option. Simply click on the toggle.



![check-in-process](/images/help/check-in-process.svg)

**Step 6:** Save Changes: Once you've made the desired selection, click on the "Save" or "Apply" button to save the changes to your badge printing settings.
