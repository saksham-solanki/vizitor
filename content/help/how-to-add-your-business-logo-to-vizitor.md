Having a personalised front desk is one of the most important features for any business. Vizitor not only helps you in securing and streamlining your business but also helps you in giving a unique identity to your front desk as well.

**Step 1:** Log into your Vizitor Dashboard.

**Step 2:** Go to the settings tab from the top panel.

**Step 3:** Click on the branding tab from the left menu.

![branding](/images/help/branding.svg)

**Step 4:** Click on the upload Image button.

![uploadImage](/images/help/uploadImage.svg)


**Note:** On the branding page, you can personalise various
aspects of your Vizitor visitor management system to reflect your company's brand identity.

**Step 5 :** Upload your business logo.

![businesslogo](/images/help/businesslogo.svg)


**Note:** Enhance your brand identity by uploading your business logo in Vizitor. This logo will be prominently displayed on the visitor check-in screen and other relevant areas, providing a professional and consistent brand experience for your visitors. Ensure that you upload a high-quality logo image to maintain the visual integrity of your brand.

By following these steps, you can easily customise the branding in Vizitor to create a cohesive and professional visitor experience. The ability to personalise the logo, colours, and email template ensures that your visitor management system aligns with your company's branding guidelines, enhancing your brand presence and creating a consistent impression for your visitors.
