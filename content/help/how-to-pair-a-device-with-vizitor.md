**Step 1:** Login to your Vizitor dashboard.

**Step 2:** Go to settings and navigate to devices.

**Step 3:** Click on the devices tab from the left menu.

![the-devices-tab](/images/help/the-devices-tab.svg)

**Step 4:**  Click on click here then use the pairing code in the visitor app.

![pairing-code](/images/help/pairing-code.svg)

**Step 5:** Now open your mobile vizitor app.

![Pair-your-device](/images/help/Pair-your-device.svg)

**Step 6:** Long press on top vizitor icon.

**Step 7:**  Then, you have to enter generated code into this field.

![pairing](/images/help/pairing.svg)

**Step 8:** If the pairing is successful, you should receive a confirmation message or notification within the app indicating that the pairing is complete.

**Step 9:** At this point, the Vizitor app should be successfully paired with the device, allowing you to access and utilize its features.

**Note:** To proceed with the next step, you will need to enter the generated code into the designated field. The generated code is typically provided by the Vizitor system and serves as a unique identifier or authentication token. This code may be sent to you via email, SMS, or displayed on a screen. 

![screen](/images/help/screen.svg)

Remember to keep the paired device connected to the internet to ensure seamless synchronisation and real-time updates with your Vizitor account.
