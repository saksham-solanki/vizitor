Creating a Vizitor account is a simple and straightforward process that can be completed in just a few steps. Vizitor is a visitor management system that helps businesses efficiently manage their visitors, making the check-in and check-out process seamless. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to create a Vizitor account as a man


**Step 1:** Visit the **Vizitor [dashboard](https://dashboard.vizitorapp.com/).**

<!-- ![dashboard](/images/help/dashboard.svg) -->

**Step 2: Sign Up**

Once you're on the Vizitor website, locate the "Sign Up" or "Get Started" button. Click on it to begin the registration process.


<!-- ![leftpanel](/images/help/leftpanel.svg) -->

**Step 3: Provide Your Information**

You'll be directed to a registration form where you need to provide your details. The typical information required includes your full name, email address, contact number, and company name (if applicable).



**Step 4: Set a Password**

Create a strong and secure password for your Vizitor account. Make sure it contains a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters for added security.


<!-- ![Pair-your-device](/images/help/Pair-your-device.svg) -->

**Step 5: Verify Your Email**

After submitting the registration form, Vizitor will send a verification email to the email address you provided during sign-up. Check your inbox for this email and click on the verification link to confirm your account.


<!-- ![Android-iOS](/images/help/Android-iOS.svg) -->

**Step 6: Log In to Your Account**

Once your email is verified, return to the Vizitor website and log in using the email address and password you set during the registration process.


**Step 7: Explore the Dashboard**

After logging in, you will be taken to the Vizitor dashboard. Take some time to familiarise yourself with the features and options available to you.


<!-- ![Scan-displayed](/images/help/Scan-displayed.svg) -->


**Step 8: Customise Your Account (Optional)**

You can customise your Vizitor account according to your preferences. For instance, you can add your company logo, set up automated messages, and customise visitor badges.

**Step 9: Start Using Vizitor**

You are now ready to use Vizitor to manage your visitors efficiently. locationYou can begin by adding your company's employees and configuring the settings based on your needs.

**How easy is it to get set up?**

Creating a Vizitor account is generally considered easy and user-friendly. The registration process is straightforward and requires only basic information. Additionally, Vizitor's interface is designed to be intuitive, making it simple for users to navigate and use the platform effectively. The dashboard and settings are organised logically, allowing users to get started quickly without much hassle. Overall, getting set up with Vizitor should be a smooth and hassle-free experience.
