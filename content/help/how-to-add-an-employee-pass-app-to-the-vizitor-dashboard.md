**Introduction:**

Adding the Employee Pass app to your visitor dashboard can improve your organisation's visitor management process and further streamline access control for your employees.

**Step 1:** Open Pass App, fill the sign up form and set Employee Pass App credentials.

![signup-with-email](/images/help/signup-with-email.svg)

**Step 2 :** If the sign up is successful, you will receive an otp mail confirming the integration.

**Step 3:** To complete the integration, you will need to provide the required credentials for the Employee Pass App. Login by generating a password.  This includes an API key or is required to link the two platforms.

![login-with-email](/images/help/login-with-email.svg)

**Step 4:** After that click on the “Add Organization” button in the Pass App, search your location (Dashboard Location) in the search bar and send the request.

![add-organisation](/images/help/add-organisation.svg)

**Step 5:** Now open the Employee tab in the dashboard and click on Employee Association at the top and accept the request.

![employee-association](/images/help/employee-association.svg)

![accept-the-request](/images/help/accept-the-request.svg)


**conclusion:**
By following this step-by-step guide, you can seamlessly integrate the Employee Pass app into your visitor dashboard, optimise your visitor management and access control processes, while providing a secure and Can ensure efficient environment.
