In today's time a health care questionnaire is a must have for your business. This not only helps you keep your employees and guests safe from any virus threat but also gives your guests extra comfort about your security measures.

**Step 1:** Login to your Visitor dashboard.

**Step 2:** Click on the settings tab from the top panel.

**Step 3:** Click on the configuration tab from the left menu.

**Step 4:** Click the Covid-19 Questionnaire tab from the top menu.

![Questionnairetabfrom](/images/help/Questionnairetabfrom.svg)

This feature enables you to create and customise questionnaires to assess the health and safety of your visitors before they enter your premises.

**Step 5:** Change the status of “Covid-19 Questionnaire?” to be required by clicking the button in front.
