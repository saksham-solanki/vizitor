**Step 1:** Login to your [Vizitor dashboard](https://dashboard.vizitorapp.com/#/login).

**Step 2:** Click on the Invites tab from the left panel.

![Inviteses](/images/help/Inviteses.svg)

**Step 3:** Click on the send invite button. After clicking a pop up form will open asking to input the mobile number of visitors you want to invite.



**Step 4:** Once you fill out all the details just click on send and the invitation will be on its way to your visitor and the entry will start appearing in dashboard.

![Inviteses](/images/help/invitationdetails.svg)

**Step 5:** You can also view the details by clicking on the View button under the Invitation tab.

**Note:** Gain a comprehensive view of visitor details by creating a "View" button under the "Invites" tab in Vizitor. This button provides easy access to visitor information, allowing you to retrieve important details such as visitor name, contact information, purpose of visit, and host details. By enabling this feature, you can conveniently and quickly review visitor information, enhancing your ability to manage and oversee visitor invitations effectively.
By following these steps, you can efficiently and effectively send invitations to visitors, ensuring a seamless and organised visitor management process with Vizitor.
