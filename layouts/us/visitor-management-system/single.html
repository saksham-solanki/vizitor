How Vizitor’s Visitor Management
System Works

A complete journey from pre-arrival to exit, designed to protect your people,
streamline the lobby, and deliver a modern visitor experience.

STEP 1: Pre-Arrival

Visitors receive a branded invite with:

Meeting details

Parking & arrival instructions

Documents to sign (NDA, HIPAA, Safety)

Unique QR code for Touchless Check-In

Outcome: Visitors arrive prepared. Employees stay undisturbed.

STEP 2: Touchless Check-In at the Lobby

Visitors scan the QR code or tap the kiosk.
In seconds, Vizitor collects:

Name

Purpose of visit

Host

Compliance confirmations

Photo (if required)

STEP 3: Instant Host Notifications

Employees receive an instant SMS, email, or push notification.

No phone calls

No chasing

No missed guests

Outcome: Meetings start on time. Employees stay focused.

STEP 4: Automatic Badge Printing

Badges print with:

Visitor photo

Visit purpose

Access level

Time of entry

Outcome: Check-ins are fast, secure, and entirely paperless.

STEP 5: Real-Time Visitor Dashboard

Front desk, security, IT, and workplace teams can see:

Who is currently inside

Who they’re here to meet

When they arrived

Expired visitors

Blocklisted individuals

Unusual activity

Outcome: A safer workplace with 100% visibility.

STEP 6: Digital Log & Automatic Checkout

Visitors check out via number or name or auto-checkout rules.

Logs are stored securely with timestamps and compliance data.

Outcome: Perfect audit trails for security, HR, and compliance teams.
