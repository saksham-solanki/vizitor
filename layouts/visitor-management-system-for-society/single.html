How can people in housing societies protect themselves?

There are a few things you can do that will keep you safe.


• Every time you touch anything outside your apartment, wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water.

• Do not touch your face, nose, eyes or mouth with unwashed hands.

• Always wears the mask when you are outside your apartment.

• Try to Cough or sneeze into your elbow or with the tissue paper.

• Avoid crowded places. Do not be part of any gatherings, no matter how small, inside your housing society.

• Stay away from anyone who seems sick in your housing society. Call them later to enquire after their health.

• If you are running a fever or have a sore throat, self isolate yourself. If the symptoms worsen, contact a healthcare professional.

• Use contact tracing app and touchless visitor management system for the check-in.

• Make sure that you are eating healthy food. Exercise regularly. These measures will bolster your immunity.

• The security guards and other staff must wear masks at all times.

• They should maintain a distance of 1 metre from people coming in from outside.
