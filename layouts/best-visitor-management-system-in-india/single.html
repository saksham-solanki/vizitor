What is a Visitor Management System?

A Visitor Management System (VMS) is software for tracking who enters a building and what they do there. It gathers details about visitors, creates ID badges, and lets staff know when someone arrives. It studies visitor information to spot any security issues. It's like an electronic front desk helper that makes entering easier, safer, and better for guests.

A visitor management system is a digital way to sign in visitors. It's like a virtual receptionist that helps you keep track of who is coming and going from your building. VMSs can also help you improve security and customer service.
