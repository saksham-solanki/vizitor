Visitor Management for Data Center

India is the second largest online market, with over 460 million plus internet users, . Booming cloud adoption is further initiating the growth in Indian data center market. Vizitor is an easy solution for safeguard of the sensitive data. With 3 easy steps you can track every step at your data center.

3 Step Process

• Tap to Check in: When the visitor comes, he/she can easily tap the ipad to check in smartly. The process is simple, paper-free and visitor friendly.

• Fill-in necessary details: After the visitor taps to check in, he/she needs to fill in some minor details about him/her like: Name, phone no., whom to visit, purpose, etc.

• Captures the face: Lastly, it just captures the face of visitor for better data management and then the visitor can easily rome around to serve their purpose.

Features

Check-in/Check-out

There is proper system for 24/7 watchguard. It records all the check-in/check-out of the guest.

Photo of visitor

The software has a feature of capturing the face of the guest for better clarity that helps in maintaining records.

Host Notifications

There is an in-built customized notification process that maintains a balance and saves time. The person (for whom there is a visitor) gets a notification so that he gets an alert.
