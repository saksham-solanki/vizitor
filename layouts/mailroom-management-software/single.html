How Mailroom management Works?

Our Mailroom Management System provides an easy and secure way to manage mail and parcels. Here's how it works

Parcel arrives and is logged in.

System matches the recipient's name.

Recipients are notified via SMS and email.

Recipient marks the package as "picked up".

Automatic reminders sent if the package isn't picked up.

Delivery status updated in system.

Our delivery management system streamlines your mailroom operations, making package retrieval hassle-free for both recipients and mailroom staff.
