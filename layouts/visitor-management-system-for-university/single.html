Touchless Visitor management system for Universities

Security serves as one of the most important aspects when it comes to keeping the university campus guarded from the unknown threats. Even as a parent or guardian of your child you would also want a safe and secure place to study. In order to have a secure premises, every university needs to make sure that it has a strong visitor management system so that the students, staff, employees and parents visiting the university needn’t bother about registering manually. Vizitor is a unique visitor management software and check-in application that not only speeds up the check-in process but also provides a modern look to your admin block’s front desk. Secure your university campus with Vizitor

• Streamlines check-in process

With Vizitor, you no longer need to check in by filling your information manually in the register. Whenever someone arrives, Vizitor captures their image which adds up to the authentication process and thus, ensures that any unauthorized person doesn’t enter the building.
