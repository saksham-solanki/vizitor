Advantages of Contactless Visitor Registration System

Digital Transformation will be the key for companies after lockdown.
More the Digitalization, more the Automation



Contactless Sign In

Visitors can use their smartphones to sign in and sign out without touching the tablet screen. You can simply check-in by scanning a visitor registration QR code. Display the secure QR code on a tablet screen or on a printed notice for visitors to scan and enter all the required information. Such visitor tracking is extremely convenient.

Pre-Register Visitors

The visitor registration system provides the facility to Pre-register your visitors using our web portal or Vizitor pass. Send invitations with an easy to use QR code to make their check-in super fast. Manage visitors and provide a smooth experience on their arrival with our Vizitor pass that is quick, and effective.

NDA Signing

You can configure different documents for different flows, capturing what you need when you need it. All documents securely stored on our cloud and available on our visitor registration software’s web portal.

Real-Time Dashboard

With the changing world, businesses need to welcome their visitors smoothly without wasting any time. Dashboards can be checked from any computer or mobile device, making it easy to keep a tab about who’s on-site. The built-in analytics dashboard provides live updates about your visitors.

Contact-tracing

Contact-tracing is one that helps to track the virus and warn staff with alerts. The contact tracing system provides check-in with adding their information, capturing a photo with a user-friendly interface. Contactless registration app uses visitor initial screening with digital questionnaires asking their health and travel history.
