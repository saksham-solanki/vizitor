Efficient and Secure: Vizitor's Digital Solution Enhances Visitor Management for Snapdeal

Vizitor has enabled Snapdeal to create a safer and more efficient workplace, while improving the overall visitor experience


Manish IT Manager, Snapdeal

Client Overview

Snapdeal is one of the largest online marketplace companies in India, serving millions of customers across the country. With over 300,000 sellers and 35 million+ products listed on their platform, Snapdeal is a major player in the e-commerce industry in India.

The Challenge

Snapdeal's offices and warehouses receive a large number of visitors every day, including job applicants, vendors, and business partners. Managing this high volume of visitors and maintaining a record of their check-in and check-out times was becoming a major challenge for Snapdeal's security and administrative staff.

Snapdeal's security team was spending a significant amount of time managing visitors' check-ins manually, leading to long wait times for visitors and affecting their overall experience.

Manish-IT Manager from Snapdeal, says Vizitor has exceeded all my expectations with its seamless and user-friendly interface. It has revolutionized the way I schedule and manage my appointments, making my life so much more organized and efficient. I am truly impressed with its innovative features and highly recommend it to anyone looking for a top-notch appointment management solution.

The Solution

Snapdeal partnered with Vizitor to streamline their visitor management system and create a better experience for their visitors.

With Vizitor's automated visitor management system, Snapdeal's visitors could easily check-in and check-out of the premises using their smartphones or self-service kiosks. Vizitor's digital solution provided a hassle-free check-in experience for visitors while reducing wait times and improving overall efficiency for Snapdeal's security and administrative staff.

Vizitor also enabled Snapdeal to maintain a digital record of all visitors, making it easier to track visitor activity and ensure compliance with security protocols.

The Results

Since implementing Vizitor's visitor management system, Snapdeal has experienced significant improvements in their overall visitor management process, including

  • Faster check-ins for visitors
  • Reduced wait times and improved visitor experience
  • Enhanced security and compliance with security protocols
  • Streamlined administrative processes and reduced workload for staff
