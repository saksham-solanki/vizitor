What Vizitor Does

Vizitor is a complete workplace management solution that enhances security and productivity throughout the office by digitizing and automating various operations.

The platform provides:

• Visitor Management - Digital check-ins, visitor verification via OTP/photo, and badge printing.

• Delivery Management - Notify employees of incoming packages and track deliveries.

• Attendance Management - Manage employee and contractor check-ins efficiently.

• Meeting Room & Space Management - Reserve, track, and optimize meeting rooms and shared workspaceusage.

• Queue & Appointment Management - Reduce waiting times and manage scheduled visits.

• Feedback System - Collect and manage visitor feedback seamlessly.

Vizitor serves organizations that need secure, contactless, and efficient workplace access management.
