Instant Notification

Nothing is better than immediately aware of your visitors and stay informed with instant notification. When a visitor enters an office, and you are greeted by a receptionist. Then receptionist would ask multiple questions about the purpose of their visit. The receptionist gets in contact with the host. Host then, either tells the receptionist to inform the visitor to wait or comes out to meet with the visitor. However, with Vizitor at your front desk, your visitors can directly enter details on the tablet, can send out a message notifying hosts of their presence.

• Automatically notify your employees when their visitors arrive. Host notifications can be sent via email, SMS and push notification.

• Vizitor makes it easy to greet your visitors. Send notifications via welcome messages.

Notifications when employees need them


Check-In Notification

The moment visitor checks-in, our visitor management system sends out an email or SMS notification to the host and visitors,

It is of following types :
1. Welcome Notification

Vizitor sends a welcome notification to visitors, when they check-in.

2. Host Notification

The host receives a notification when their visitors have arrived.

Check-In Notification

When the visitor check-out, our visitor management system sends out an email and SMS notification to the host and visitors.

It is of two types :
1. Thanks Notification

When visitors signs-out, they receive a thanks message.

2. Host Notification

The host receives a notification when the visitors checks-out.

Push Notifications

The Vizitor PASS mobile app can help your host to send invites to their visitors at any time. It can send push notifications that include visitor photos and sign-in details. You can even send your visitors a quick response, right from the app.
