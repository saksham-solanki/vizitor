Instant Visitor Notifications for Faster Check-Ins

Never keep your guests waiting. Vizitor instantly alerts hosts when a visitor arrives, saving time and boosting efficiency

Delays at the front desk can disrupt meetings and create a poor visitor experience. With instant visitor notifications, Vizitor ensures that hosts are immediately informed through email, SMS, or mobile app alerts the moment a guest checks in.

This real-time communication streamlines check-ins, reduces waiting time, and helps organizations maintain a professional and welcoming environment. Whether it’s a corporate office, hospital, school, or event venue, instant notifications eliminate confusion and keep operations running smoothly.

Notifications when employees need them


Check-In Notification

The moment visitor checks-in, our visitor management system sends out an email or SMS notification to the host and visitors,

It is of following types :
1. Welcome Notification

Vizitor sends a welcome notification to visitors, when they check-in.

2. Host Notification

The host receives a notification when their visitors have arrived.

Check-In Notification

When the visitor check-out, our visitor management system sends out an email and SMS notification to the host and visitors.

It is of two types :
1. Thanks Notification

When visitors signs-out, they receive a thanks message.

2. Host Notification

The host receives a notification when the visitors checks-out.

Push Notifications

The Vizitor PASS mobile app can help your host to send invites to their visitors at any time. It can send push notifications that include visitor photos and sign-in details. You can even send your visitors a quick response, right from the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are instant visitor notifications?

Instant visitor notifications are real-time alerts sent to hosts or staff the moment a visitor checks in, ensuring faster communication and improved visitor management.

How does Vizitor send instant notifications?

Vizitor sends notifications via multiple channels: SMS, email, or mobile app push notifications, so hosts are immediately informed when their guest arrives.

Who can benefit from instant visitor notifications?

Organizations of all types: offices, schools, hospitals, events, government offices, co-working spaces, and manufacturing plants benefit from real-time visitor alerts.

How do instant notifications improve security?

By alerting hosts in real time, Vizitor reduces the risk of unattended visitors, ensures accurate visitor logs, and supports compliance and audit requirements.

Can notifications be customized?

Yes, Organizations can choose notification channels, templates, and recipient lists based on their workflow and security requirements.

How fast are instant visitor notifications?

Notifications are sent immediately upon visitor check-in, typically within seconds, ensuring hosts never miss a guest.

Do instant notifications work for pre-registered visitors?

Yes, Whether a visitor is pre-registered or checks in on arrival, hosts receive instant alerts for seamless coordination.
