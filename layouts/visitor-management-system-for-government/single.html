Visitor Management System For Government

• High security is an essential requirement when it comes to government work. This is because governments have to deal with sensitive and confidential documents. Hence, identification of visitors becomes necessary.

• Deploying a security personnel outside government offices or organizations is not enough to track and restrict visitors. This makes it essential to set up a visitor management system. Thus, in order to make your government office/organization intruder-proof, we have a solution, just for you!

• Vizitor, a visitor management software, gives you professional check-in system that is simple to use and is a lot more professional than the conventional manual registration in sheets.

• Our check-in ecosystem consists of the Vizitor installed in your device, placed at the reception desk for guests to check in, for pre-registrations and the Vizitor Dashboard for your admin team to keep a bird’s eye view on the visitor’s movement.

• Once the visitor checks-in through the Vizitor filling basic details like name, email address, mobile number, host name etc., a notification is sent to the host consisting of the visitor’s details. Vizitor also offers printed badges for the visitors after they check-in which makes the identification process simple and easy. This eventually will reduce the risk of threats like robbery and corruption.

Some of the benefits of installing visitor management system at government offices or agencies are as follows

• Track visitors and prevents unauthorized access.

• Quick and easy sign-in

• Secure identification

• Reliable data access.

• Tighten security

• Government offices or agencies should not be restricted to old conventional methods of tracking and managing visitors, instead they should power up for a modern, quick and highly customisable visitor management system like Vizitor for smooth and streamlined operation.
