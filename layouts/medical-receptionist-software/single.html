How a Digital Visitor Management System can help the Hospital reception area?

Many hospitals in India still use a paper-based system for managing visitors. The dimensions and complexity of security and operational challenges the hospitals face due to this paper-based system make it imperative that they need a strong and efficient hospital operations management system in place. One such solution is a digital visitor management software for hospitals to make sure security within the hospital facility is efficient. Replacing paper registers or semi-automated systems that need high maintenance hardware the digital VMSs are the future. However, its adoption across the healthcare industry, mainly in India, has not yet begun.

Managing Visitors

Since hospitals receive a lot of visitors on a day to day basis, it becomes imperative for hospital security and facility managers to possess a track of visitors on a daily basis.

• A visitor management system also recognizes different categories of visitors and captures maximum visitor data alongside visitor photos and standard details like email id and phone numbers, within seconds.

•This way, hospitals can prevent unlawful activities on the premises. All records are accessible on a single dashboard and may be viewed in real-time also.

•Another additional security measure you get with VMS is that the software is definitely scalable to many locations and these locations can be managed from one location or dashboard.

•It can assist you schedule appointments via the dashboard you can also send alerts and notifications to the host and therefore the visitor. Making the whole visitor process seamless.

•It also can send alerts in case of an emergency like an earthquake or evacuation. You can notify all the visitors to vacate the building as soon as possible.

•This comes extremely handy especially when hospitals in seismically active areas are deploying quake-ready technology to stay them safe in an emergency.

Improve your Hospital’s Brand & Reputation

Having a visitor management Software at the front desk enables automated check-in processes which result in a decrease within the pressure of handling visitors, eventually leading to building the reputation and brand image of hospitals, which is important for highly competitive hospital industry.

•A good system can create mutual dependency and trust between the hospital management and visitors. Also, the system will impress visitors which could directly or indirectly affect your brand reputation.

•Visitors understand that your hospital is more concerned about visitor security and also about hospital management.

•Naturally, it builds mutual trust between the visitors and hospital management, and also it results in building a brand reputation within the visitor’s mind.

Improve Work Efficiency and Patient Satisfaction

According to a recent survey, 45% of individuals said they prefer to have a VMS for his or her organization to enhance work efficiency.

•A sophisticated visitor app has advanced self-service functions. Such a system offers a patient or the patient attendant the power to feature his or her own list of Visitors to the system.

•The visitors can then get automatic access to go to the patient during visiting hours.

•The system also enables the patient or the attendant to spot someone as an unwanted visitor. This empowers the patient and goes an extended way in enhancing patient satisfaction.

•Self-registration software can lessen the burden on hospital receptionists who are usually overburdened with managing visitors.

• The modern digital visitor management system enables visitors to manually enter their details and schedule the appointment with doctors and provides a seamless experience for the visitors.

• The QR code-based visitor id within the VMS gives a seamless check-in experience for the visitors.

•The self-registration visitor management system is an efficient way of handling visitors, especially, pre-registered visitors, and can reduce the manual check-ins that receptionists handle.

•Having a VMS at your front desk can reduce pressure on hospital receptionists who are mostly overburdened by managing visitors thereby improving their work efficiency by leaps and bounds.
