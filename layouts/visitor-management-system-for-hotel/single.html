Powerful Features of Vizitor: Hotel Check-in Software that Does It All

QR Code Sign-in: Instant Access for Guests

The Vizitor check-in software automatically generates unique QR codes for each guest upon booking. When guests arrive at your hotel, they can simply scan their QR code on their smartphones to complete the check-in process without needing to interact with the front desk. This ensures a seamless, contact-free experience from start to finish.

Mobile Number Verification: Extra Layer of Security

Ensuring guest safety is a top priority. Vizitor strengthens your hotel’s security with mobile number verification. Each guest must verify their phone number using a secure OTP (One-Time Password), adding a layer of protection before they even step into your hotel. This ensures that the guest’s identity is confirmed, minimizing the risk of fraudulent check-ins.
